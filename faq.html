How does your design process work?

Our Process starts with the Client explaining what type of graphics you require (for print or digital use) and providing similar projects to show us your vision. From this requirement, which we call The Brief, initial design concepts are developed, which you can choose from and adjust to your final needs. Completed projects will be sent via email or an external file-sharing service.

What design services do you provide?

We design everything from logos, flyers, brochures, and banners.

What are your payment terms?

For all small projects and designs, we always need upfront payment. For more advanced projects and complex artworks, a 60% deposit is required before any design work commences. The final payment should be made when your files are ready to be sent to you.

In what format will I receive my designs?

We will provide you with AI, EPS, PDF and JPG files for vector files. You will receive JPG, PNG and PDF files for raster files.

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept payment in the form of cash, cheque or electronic transfer./p>

Who owns the copyright for the designs you produce?

Copyright for all work done by Image and Likeness Studio transfers to the Client as soon as the balance is fully settled. However, we reserve the right to use any work for promotional purposes.

How long will it take to deliver designs?

Projects will be finished within three work days of the initial payment being completed for the commencement of the project.

What type of Photography do you specialize in?

We specialize in photography services for professional portraits, Business headshots, and commercial and advertising shoots.

Which locations do you provide photography services?

We provide photography services in Connecticut, USA and House no. 14 Weija -Tetegu Accra, Ghana

What are the charges for Photography?

It differs from the types of photography services we provide. We, however, have rate cards for our studio photography services, which can be made available based on request.

What are your payment terms?

Full payment is required to book the session.

How long will it take to deliver photos?

Retouching and editing will be finished within three work days after the session.

WWhat is the Process for choosing Photos to be edited?

The Client selects preferred photos with the photographer, which will be retouched and delivered as final work.

Who owns the copyright for the photos you produce?

Image copyrights remain the property of IMLKSTUDIO; as such, images may be used solely for advertisement on our website or social media.

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on what you need. Building a 1000-page mega site takes longer than a smaller eight-page website.

What size companies do you produce web designs for?

We like to work for personal brands, startups or agencies because our Process remains the same; we care about your vision and place to understand the needs of growing organizations.

Do you redesign existing websites?

We certainly do! That is, if the desired result for redesign is discussed and understood.

Do you work internationally?

Yes, we do! The advent of online conference tools like Skype, Google Meet, and Zoom, to name a few, makes working with businesses anywhere in the world easy.

What are the payment terms for the design and development of my website?

We like to work on a 60% upfront basis with an outstanding 40% payable after the development is completed.

Do you offer ongoing help with our new site once it's launched?

Should a client need our help with web support and maintenance, we'll be here for you.

Will my website work on smartphones and tablets as well?

It certainly will! We understand the trends of more people accessing the web via small screens than ever, making mobile responsive web design essential to business growth. So, every website we create is mobile responsive.

Can I purchase my domain?

Yes, you can! We encourage our clients to purchase their domain. The reason is that it makes renewing your hosting by yourself easy. And, of course, we help you to choose the best.

What is web hosting?

Web hosting is required to get your website online. It is the space you 'rent' that houses your web files and allows users to access your site and its information.
